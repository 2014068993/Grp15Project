THE FOOD BIBLE



The best finger food: Olive crostini
April 20, 2016 | Eduard Somera 

Prep time: 15 minutes
Cook time: 2 minutes
Yield: Makes approximately 24 toasts


Ingredients


  • 1/2 cup black olives
    • 

  • 1/2 cup green olives with pimientos
    • 

  • 2 medium cloves garlic
    • 

  • 1/2 cup fresh grated Parmesan cheese
    • 

  • 4 Tbsp butter
    • 

  • 2 Tbsp olive oil
    • 

  • 1/2 cup Monterey jack cheese, grated
    • 

  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
    • 

  • 1 baguette
    • 

Method
      

    1. Chop the olives in a food processor. Transfer to medium bowl. With machine running, 
drop garlic through the feed tube of the food processor and mince. Add Parmesan, butter,
and olive oil, and process into a paste.
      2. 

    2. Add butter mixture to bowl with olives. Fold in jack cheese and parsley.
      3. 

    3. Cut baguette into thin slices and spread each slice generously with olive mixture
      4. 

    4. Cook under broiler until bubbly and lightly browned - about 2 minutes.
      5. 

    5. Serve right out of oven. Helps to make olive mixture in advance and spread right before
you put in the oven.
      6.
